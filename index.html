About

I am an incoming graduate student at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign. Previously, I was an undergraduate student at Rutgers University until 2026. As an undergraduate, I participated in the 2025 Clemson University REU in Number Theory and the 2024 DIMACS REU.

Within mathematics, I am primarily interested in analysis. More specifically, I would describe my interests as being in harmonic analysis and its applications, including those to analytic number theory and partial differential equations.
